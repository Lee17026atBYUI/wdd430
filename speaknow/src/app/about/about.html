Welcome!

Speak now or forever hold your peace.


Use this web app to find suggestions for people to give talks in Sacrament Meeting. This is just based on the longest time since they last spoke.

When adding a new member, only the name (minimum 3 characters) is required.

If they accepted the invitation, you can quickly mark that they gave a talk (pretend it's today).

If someone is not currently interested, you can bump them down the list. They will show up again later (3 months).
